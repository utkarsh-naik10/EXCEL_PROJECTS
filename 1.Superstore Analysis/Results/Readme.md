## 📊 Analysis & Insights

### 🔹 **1. Sales Analysis**
**Files:** `Sales Analysis.jpg` & `Sales Analysis 2.jpg`

- **Total Sales:** $2.26 Million across 2015–2018.  
- **Yearly Growth:** Consistent upward trend — from $480K (2015) to $720K (2018).  
- **Average Order Value:** $230.77, showing stable per-order spending.  
- **Top Categories by Revenue:**
  - **Technology:** 37%  
  - **Furniture:** 32%  
  - **Office Supplies:** 31%  
- **Top Sub-Categories:** Phones, Chairs, Tables, and Storage drive the majority of sales.
- **Top Customer Segments:**  
  - **Consumer Segment** contributes over **50%** of total revenue.  
  - **Corporate** (30%) and **Home Office** (19%) follow.  

📈 *Insight:* Technology products yield the highest returns, suggesting strong market demand and higher margins in this category.

---

### 🗺️ **2. Regional Analysis**
**File:** `Regional Analysis.jpg`

- **Top Performing Region:** **West** ($7.1M), followed by **East** ($6.65M).  
- **Top States:**  
  - **California** ($446K)  
  - **New York** ($306K)  
  - **Texas** ($168K)  
- **City with Most Orders:** **New York City** (891 orders).  
- **Average Order Value by Region:**
  - **South:** $243.52 (Highest)
  - **East:** $243.41
  - **West:** $226.18
  - **Central:** $216.36  

🌎 *Insight:* Western and Eastern regions dominate sales, with the South showing stronger order profitability per transaction.

---

### 🚚 **3. Logistics & Shipping Analysis**
**File:** `Logistics Analysis.jpg`

- **Average Delivery Time:** 4 days overall.  
- **Most Used Shipping Mode:** Standard Class (60% of orders).  
- **Faster Shipping & Sales:**
  - *First Class* and *Same Day* contribute higher sales per order.  
- **Top Delay States:**
  - District of Columbia, Maine, and Wyoming — ~5 days average.  
- **Delivery Speed Summary:**
  - **Orders delivered under 3 days:** 32%  
  - **Orders delivered over 3 days:** 68%  

🚀 *Insight:* While Standard Class dominates, switching more customers to *First Class* shipping could improve satisfaction and speed without heavily impacting cost.

---

### ⏱️ **4. Time & Seasonality Trends**
**File:** `Time Trend.jpg`

- **Peak Sales Months:** November, December, and September.  
- **Seasonality:** Sales rise significantly in **Q4** (holiday period).  
- **Quarterly Growth Pattern:**  
  - Q1 → Q2 → Q3 → Q4 shows consistent upward trend.  
- **Yearly Comparison (2015–2018):** Clear YoY growth with **2018 outperforming by 50%+**.

📆 *Insight:* The Superstore experiences end-of-year spikes — aligning marketing and inventory planning during Q4 can maximize revenue.

---

### 👥 **5. Customer Insights**
**File:** `Customer Insight.jpg`

- **Unique Customers:** 794 total.  
- **Average Sales per Customer:** $1,346.  
- **Top Buyers:** Mitch Willingham, Sean Miller, Tamara Chand, and Grant Thornton.  
- **Customer Segmentation:**
  - Consumer segment has the highest spenders overall.
- **Multi-Category Behavior:**
  - **97%** of customers purchase from **multiple product categories**, showing cross-selling success.

💡 *Insight:* Repeat and cross-category purchases are high, suggesting strong brand loyalty and a well-diversified product strategy.

## 🧭 Recommendations

1. **Marketing Focus:**  
   - Promote **Technology** and **Furniture** categories during Q4 (holiday & year-end periods).  
   - Leverage consumer segment targeting for highest returns.

2. **Regional Strategy:**  
   - Strengthen operations in **West and East regions** to sustain high sales.  
   - Explore growth in **Central region**, which shows the lowest performance.

3. **Shipping Optimization:**  
   - Improve **Standard Class** logistics efficiency.  
   - Encourage **First Class** usage for high-value orders.

4. **Customer Retention:**  
   - Implement loyalty programs for repeat customers.  
   - Offer bundle deals across multiple categories.
